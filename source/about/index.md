# 👨‍💻 About Jing Li

Hi, I'm Jing Li.
A Computer Science graduate from Shanxi University, and an AI & Full-Stack Developer seeking the optimal solution in code logic, macro-systems, and a minimalist lifestyle. I am currently working at **TAL Education Group**

I've always believed that whether you are building high-concurrency web applications or trying to make sense of complex social and financial systems, the most elegant solutions are always rooted in a profound understanding of the underlying logic.

---

### `// 01. Tech Domain: Code, Compute & The Terminal`

My daily work lives at the intersection of modern Web architecture and Artificial Intelligence. I am passionate about making technology run faster, lighter, and smarter.

* **Full-Stack Architecture**: A steadfast advocate for modern, highly optimized frontend ecosystems. I love building fast, dynamic web applications centered around **React, Vite, and the TanStack ecosystem**.
* **AI Agents & LLM Inference**: Deeply focused on optimizing Large Language Model (LLM) inference. From researching Speculative Decoding to exploring the Model Context Protocol (MCP), my goal is to seamlessly integrate AI into real-world business scenarios.
* **Linux & Terminal Purist**: My digital universe is powered by Linux and constructed with Zsh, Tmux, and Neovim. I am obsessed with solving problems entirely within the command line, fully enjoying the instant feedback of a pure keyboard-driven workflow.
* **Hardcore Compute & HPC Roots**: Forged during my time in the SXU-HPC (High-Performance Computing) team—where we secured **3rd place at IndySCC 24**—my obsession with hardware performance runs deep. Today, my "private lab" is anchored by a custom-built workstation running **Fedora Server**, equipped with an RTX 3090 Ti. It routinely runs the Qwen 3.5 27B model locally to validate my wildest ideas.

### `// 02. Cognitive Domain: Multidimensional Systems & Mental Models`

If writing code is about building the rules of the digital world, I am equally obsessed with reverse-engineering the laws of the real world. I dedicate a massive amount of bandwidth to cross-disciplinary cognitive reconstruction:

* **Radical Truth & Operating Systems**: My personal and professional "operating system" has been profoundly shaped by Ray Dalio's ***Principles***. I heavily rely on radical truth, transparency, and systematic decision-making to navigate both work and life.
* **Finance & Market Game Theory**: I have a strong interest in stock trading, mutual funds, and macro-finance. I view financial markets as a massive, real-world algorithm heavily driven by game theory, and I try to understand the objective laws of economic cycles through investing and macro-analysis.
* **Social Sciences & Philosophy**: Deeply immersed in history, politics, sociology, and psychology. I love finding the intersections between grand historical processes and micro-psychological mechanisms, constantly iterating on my mental models.

### `// 03. Offline Life: Minimalism & White Space`

When the screens turn off, my offline life defaults to absolute minimalism:

* **Flow State BGM**: The neo-classical piano compositions of Virginio Aiello and Ludovico Einaudi are the absolute triggers for my deep-thinking and flow states.
* **Minimalist Energy Consumption**: When it comes to fueling my body, I reject heavy, high-fat, meat-heavy diets. A simple, home-cooked plate of stir-fried cabbage and potatoes is more than enough to fully recharge my system.
